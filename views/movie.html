Movie Night

Enjoy summer evenings under the big screen. This summer the Town of Monument and U.S. Taekwondo Center will present outdoor movie nights. These are free to the public and will take place at the Tri-Lakes YMCA, Jackson Creek at the Monument Marketplace Clocktower (between Walmart & Home Depot), and Dirty Woman Creek Park. Everyone is invited to bring chairs and blankets to enjoy a movie under the stars. Activities begin around 7 and will include demonstrations by U.S. Taekwondo Center, board breaking & a bounce house! Movies will begin at dark (approx 8:30). Please bring your family and friends, but leave your pets at home.

Details

Date: June 14, 2019
Time: 7:00pm
Location: Tri-Lakes YMCA
Hall Address: 17250 Jackson Creek Parkway, Monument, CO 80132
Contact: 719-884-8013
Cost: Free
